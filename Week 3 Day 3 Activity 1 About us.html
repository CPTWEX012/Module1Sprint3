    • History

    Mzamomtsha Primary School was built in 1999,it is a public primary school located at 49 Valangentambo Street. It is one of the public primary schools in urban Driftsands suburb. It is a Section 21 school with ABE functions and classified under quintile 3 as a no fee institution. In 2014 there were 293 registered learners, including 51 Grade R learners. In the year 2016, the school had 359 learners served by 7 teachers, with a student teacher ratio of 52:1.Mzamomtsha Primary School is a valid, registered and prosperous school under the leadership of the Principal Sg Kuze, 9 teachers and 530 learners.

    Can students start in Mzamomtsha primary school at 7th grade?

    Yes, one can apply at the school from any grade. We need the relevant documents from the previous school.


    How is security handled?

    We have burglar alarms in place on both windows and doors, 24-hour security company which monitors all school equipment's and safety of our students.

    ​

    What happens if students get sick or injured?

    As a parent, you can support your student's health and well-being, but keep in mind that we have teachers who know first aid and can help when needed.
